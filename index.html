📌 Project Overview

The Phishing Awareness Simulator is designed to educate users about phishing attacks, social engineering techniques, malicious links, fake login pages, and email scams. The goal is to improve security awareness and reduce the risk of credential theft.

🎯 Objectives

  • Identify phishing attacks.
  • Recognize suspicious emails.
  • Understand social engineering tactics.
  • Improve cybersecurity awareness.
  • Promote safe online behavior.

📧 Phishing Simulation Scenarios

  • Banking Alert Scam
  • Password Reset Scam
  • Prize Winner Scam
  • Fake Login Portal
  • Business Email Compromise

⚠️ Common Warning Signs

  • Unknown sender addresses.
  • Urgent requests for action.
  • Unexpected attachments.
  • Suspicious links and URLs.
  • Requests for passwords or sensitive information.

🔒 Security Recommendations

  • Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA).
  • Verify email senders before responding.
  • Never click suspicious links.
  • Use strong unique passwords.
  • Report phishing attempts immediately.
  • Keep systems and software updated.
